What is STEAM Education?

STEAM education integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics into a cohesive learning approach. It is not just about teaching these subjects individually but exploring the connections between them. At NTPL, we recognize the importance of nurturing well-rounded individuals who can seamlessly apply their knowledge across diverse disciplines.
