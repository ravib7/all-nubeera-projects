Echo is a whole new way to look at the Business of Revenue Cycle.

The echo automation suite comprises of the following:

echobot is a complete business process automation suite that allows process experts to build bots on the go. With a dynamic design studio, echobot's comprehensive library of pre-built micro-bots, process managers can drag-and-drop the required functionalities to create custom bots. No wonder, echobot is the solution of choice for revenue cycle processes.

For decades now, revenue cycle professionals have grappled with the problem of automating medical coding processes. With multiple autonomous coding platforms that are part of our solution set, you can generate medical codes from clinical documentation quickly and automatically. These engines utilize unified medical language systems (UMLS), leading-edge Natural Language Processing (NLP) technology, and top-of-the-notch encoders. In addition, underlying technologies such as OCR, AI, and Machine Learning improve coding accuracy, reduce labor costs, and optimize reimbursements.

Experience 2.5x productivity improvement with the industry's most robust payments automation platform, echopay. Access Healthcare currently processes over 100 M manual payment transactions through this engine, with about 85% of transactions not requiring human intervention. echopay utilizes multiple OCR engines for character perfection, machine-learning-powered forms recognition, and artificial intelligence to reconcile payments at a high processing accuracy rate to deliver a near straight-through processing experience.

Let's face it. Your A/R, denial management, and eligibility verification call centers require costly resources. What if you had a way to make every minute count and not waste these resources on mundane tasks? echorev brings you solutions to obtain claim status automatically, reduce prior authorization processing burden, enhance focus on denial resolution by using decision trees, reduce manual effort in filing appeals, and even make decisions on follow-up actions, classify the claims as callable and non-callable claims using the proprietary Machine learning solution. echorev ensures that you work on the right claims at the right time and takes unnecessary pressure out of your revenue cycle call center.

As many as 96% of the healthcare CFOs feel that they need better financial and operational data analytics to optimize their revenue cycle. Built on HFMA MAP keys, an industry-standard definition of different revenue cycle KPIs, echosmrt lets you take back control of your revenue cycle. We offer multiple solutions to run reports, aggregate data, and create smart dashboards that let you make decisions intuitively.
